# Stay Safe Online – My Personal Safety Checklist

## General Safety

- [ ] I use different passwords for different accounts and 
      never reuse the same one twice
- [ ] I have turned on two-step verification on my Gmail, 
      Instagram and banking apps
- [ ] I never click on random links sent on WhatsApp or SMS 
      even if they look official
- [ ] Before entering details on any website I check if the 
      URL looks right and has https
- [ ] I log out of my accounts when using someone else's 
      device or a college computer
- [ ] I keep my phone and apps updated because old versions 
      have security holes

## UPI and Money Safety

- [ ] I now know that a UPI collect request takes money FROM 
      me — it is never a way to receive money
- [ ] I will never share my UPI PIN or OTP with anyone on 
      call even if they claim to be from my bank or NPCI
- [ ] I double check the name and UPI ID before sending 
      any payment to someone
- [ ] I have set a daily limit on my UPI app so even if 
      something goes wrong the damage is limited

## Social Media Safety

- [ ] I don't post my location or daily routine publicly
- [ ] I keep my Instagram and Snapchat on private mode
- [ ] I don't accept follow or friend requests from 
      people I don't actually know

## If Something Goes Wrong

Report it immediately — the faster you act the better

- Website: cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930 (free, available 24x7)
- Also call your bank's fraud helpline right away
- Save all screenshots and transaction IDs as proof
