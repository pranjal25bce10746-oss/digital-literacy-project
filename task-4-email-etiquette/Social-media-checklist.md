# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## DO's
- Keep your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles professional and updated
- Think before you post — ask yourself if your professor or future employer would be comfortable seeing it
- Use strong unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication on all social accounts
- Credit others when you share or repost their work or ideas
- Report cyberbullying or harassment using the platform's report tools
- Review your privacy settings regularly

## DON'Ts
- Don't share your real-time location publicly on any platform
- Don't post during exams or classes in a way that violates college rules
- Don't engage in or ignore online harassment or hate speech
- Don't share confidential information about your college or classmates
- Don't accept friend requests from people you don't know personally
- Don't express frustration about professors or college publicly by name
- Don't share exam questions or copyrighted materials online
